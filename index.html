About me

I am currently a R&D Engineer at Montimage, an innovative company located in Paris.

I received in 2012 my engineering degree in Information and Communication Systems at Hanoi University of Science and Technology (Vietnam) and the Master degree at UPMC-Paris 6 in 2013. In 2016, I was honored the title Doctorate of Telecom SudParis /Univeristy Paris Saclay.

My research/working interests include Security Monitoring, IoT/Sensors Network Security, Intrusion and Security Violation Detection.
